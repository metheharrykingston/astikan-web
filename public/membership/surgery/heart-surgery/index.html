Our Procedures

Advanced cardiac procedures performed by our expert team

Coronary Angioplasty & Stenting
Interventional

Minimally invasive procedure to open blocked heart arteries and restore blood flow.

⏱️ Duration: 1-2 hours 🛌 Recovery: 1-2 days
Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG)
Surgical

Surgical procedure to create new routes for blood flow around blocked arteries.

⏱️ Duration: 3-6 hours 🛌 Recovery: 4-6 weeks
Heart Valve Replacement
Surgical

Replacement of damaged heart valves with mechanical or biological valves.

⏱️ Duration: 2-4 hours 🛌 Recovery: 4-6 weeks
Pacemaker Implantation
Interventional

Device implantation to regulate heart rhythm and prevent slow heartbeats.

⏱️ Duration: 1-2 hours 🛌 Recovery: 1-2 days
Catheter Ablation
Interventional

Treatment for abnormal heart rhythms using radiofrequency energy.

⏱️ Duration: 2-4 hours 🛌 Recovery: 1-2 days
TAVR (Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement)
Interventional

Minimally invasive valve replacement without open-heart surgery.

⏱️ Duration: 2-3 hours 🛌 Recovery: 3-5 days
